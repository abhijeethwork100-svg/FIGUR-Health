About Aktive

  • How can I be sure that Aktive is a legitimate medical provider?

    Aktive is a health platform that connects you with health professionals such as doctors and pharmacists. Aktive verifies each professional to give you the comfort of knowing you're in good hands.

  • Why does Aktive not mention medication by name?

    We can't mention medications by name due to advertising restrictions. However, our trustworthy partner doctors can decide (with you) which treatment plan works best. Take our quiz today.

  • How does Aktive guarantee my security and privacy?

    Aktive uses up-to-date technology to keep your information entirely confidential. We guarantee to keep your data safe from third parties unless you provide consent. Your medical information will only be viewed by the partner doctor reviewing your consultation.

Medical consultation

  • Why do I need to do the medical consultation?

    The information you provide during the medical consultation will allow a doctor registered with our platform to assess your information and suggest a treatment to help you on your weight loss journey.

  • When do I qualify for your treatment?

    You may qualify for treatment if:

    • You have a BMI of 30 or above
    • You have a BMI of 27 or above and also have an existing weight-related medical condition (such has high blood pressure or high cholesterol).
    • You aren't taking another medication that might interact with Orlistat or a GLP-1.

  • What is BMI?

    Body Mass Index (BMI) is calculated using your height and weight. It tells you whether you're underweight, a healthy weight, or overweight based on your height, age, and gender.

  • Why do you need a body image?

    A body image is required to ensure that you are eligible and so that we can see your progress. We will only ask for you to provide this once every 3 months. The picture will only be visible to you and our partner doctor - no one else will have access to it. We realise it's inconvenient, but it's essential for the safety of our patients and to protect those who are vulnerable.

  • Do I know immediately if I qualify for treatment?

    After finishing the consultation, you will receive a call from an allied healthcare professional to confirm your details. During our working hours, one of our partner doctors will review your answers within 1-2 hours. Outside working hours, you can expect to hear back from us on the following working day. If we have any further questions or are unable to prescribe to you, we will send through a message.

  • Why wasn’t my subscription approved?

    If your subscription isn't approved by one of our partner doctors, this means that the treatment isn't safe for you. It is our number one priority to keep you from any potential health risks.

  • What happens if my subscription is not approved?

    If you're not approved for treatment, we will not take any form of payment. Your subscription will be cancelled and any pending transactions will bounce back into your account. There will be no hidden charges.

  • Do I have to consult my local doctor?

    When starting a new treatment, it's best to inform your local doctor about the changes to your medical situation. This way they can also keep track of your medical history.

  • Which dosage will I receive and why?

    When starting treatment, you will begin on the lowest dosage. This helps to minimise any initial side effects as your body gets used to taking the new medication. As the weeks go by, your body becomes adjusted to the medication and you can increase your dosage slowly.

  • What is the delivery frequency?

  • How does the dosage increase work?

    After 4 weeks of taking the same dose, your body will have adjusted enough for you to increase it. Taking a higher dose means more medicine, and therefore more weight loss progress. You may experience mild side effects every time you increase your dose - this is entirely normal and will subside once your body gets used to the new dosage. If you want to stay on the same dose, this is also possible. Just get in touch.

  • Do I have to update my medical answers or body image?

Treatment

  • How does it work?

    Our treatments, available as both tablets and injection, work in five simple ways:

    • When the body releases too much sugar into the blood, they help the body release more insulin to curb sugar levels.
    • They help the body remove excessive sugar from the blood.
    • They stop the liver from making and releasing further sugar into the blood.
    • They signal the brain to eat less, thereby suppressing appetite and unnecessary bingeing.
    • They slow down the speed at which the food leaves the stomach. This helps lose weight over time.

  • Why is it more effective than other weight loss medications?

    Our treatments work on many levels. The active ingredient works by making your body produce more of a specific hormone that affects how hungry you feel.

    This slows down digestion and stomach emptying. It affects your appetite because your brain gets the signal "I'm full' much quicker. Which means you eat less.

    The medication also resets your metabolism. You have a set point that your body defends to maintain. It gives you hunger signals if you drop below this point. The hormones reset this point to a lower level.

    Our treatments are clinically proven to help you lose weight. They are prescribed with utmost discretion and caution by renowned doctors and specialists - this is something that cannot be said of other weight loss medications.

  • What are the benefits?

    The main benefit of our treatment is that it's proven to effectively cause weight loss.

    When combined with a healthy lifestyle:

    • Individuals using our weight loss tablets can lose up to 15% of their original body weight within a year.
    • Individuals using our weight loss injection can lose up to 21% of their original body weight within 16 months.

    Losing weight also reduces your risk of developing severe health complications later in life, such as heart disease, high cholesterol and strokes.

  • How much weight can I lose on your weight loss plan?

    How much weight you can lose depends on the individual and is also dependent on factors such as diet and exercise.

    However, on average:

    • Individuals using our weight loss tablets can lose up to 15% of their original body weight within a year.
    • Individuals using our weight loss injection can lose up to 21% of their original body weight within 16 months.

  • How is it guaranteed that the medicines are original?

    All our treatments are dispensed by accredited partner pharmacies.

  • Who can use it?

    We can only provide the treatments to individuals with a BMI of 30 and above (or 27 and above with weight-related health conditions). If you meet that criteria, fill out our online medical quiz. One of our partner doctors will then check your medical history and determine if you're suitable.

  • When do I start seeing results?

    Everybody's weight loss journey looks slightly different. However, you will likely start seeing results as soon as one month into treatment. Remember, healthy weight loss takes time and patience.

  • Do I need to go on a diet?

    Yes. For the drug to work, you must make the necessary lifestyle changes to your diet and exercise habits. You should consume fewer calories daily, and make changes to your exercise regime. Most people should exercise 75 - 150 minutes weekly - this could be as simple as walking or doing household chores.

Daily tablet

  • What is the daily tablet?

    The daily tablet is a new generation weight loss medication that is taken orally once a day. No need to inject yourself.

  • How do I take the tablet?

    The tablet is taken every morning on an empty stomach. Remember to take it first thing and wait 30 minutes before your first meal or drink of the day.

  • What dosages are available?

    The daily tablet is available in 3 different doses: 3 mg, 7 mg, and 14 mg.

  • How do I increase dosages?

    After 4 weeks of treatment, we will automatically increase your dose (as long as it's been deemed suitable by one of our partner doctors, and you're happy to take the next dosage level).

  • What if I miss a dose?

    If you miss a dose, skip your tablet for that day and wait until the following morning to take another one.

  • What if I experience side effects?

    It's very common to experience mild side effects, especially when you first start treatment and increase your dose. These can be managed with diet - for example, fatty and sugary foods are more likely to cause nausea.

Weekly Injection

  • What is the weekly injection?

    The injection we prescribe is a prescription-only, once-weekly injection that is used for weight management.

    It removes excess sugar from the blood and slows digestion, which makes you feel fuller for longer and lose weight.

  • How does the injection work for weight loss?

    The active ingredient in the injection works in five simple ways:

    • When the body releases too much sugar into the blood, it helps the body release more insulin to curb sugar levels.
    • It helps the body remove excessive sugar from the blood.
    • It stops the liver from making and releasing further sugar into the blood.
    • It signals the brain to eat less, thereby suppressing appetite and unnecessary bingeing.
    • It slows down the speed at which the food leaves the stomach. This helps lose weight over time.

  • What should I do before taking the injection?

    Here’s what you should do before taking the injection:

    • Inspect the medicine inside the pen carefully before using
      The liquid in the pen should be clear and colourless to slightly yellow.
    • Check the name and coloured label
      This will help you ensure you are taking the right injection and the correct dosage prescribed to you.
    • Place the injection pen with the needle on a clean surface
    • Wash your hands with warm water and soap
    • Pull off the pen cap
    • Wipe the red inner seal with an alcohol swab
    • Attach a new needle to the pen firmly using a twisting motion
    • Remove the outer and inner needle shields
      Discard the inner shield and keep the outer needle shield. You’ll need it later.

  • How do I prepare the injection?

    Here is how you can prepare the injection:

    • Turn the dose knob until you hear 2 clicks and the prime position lines up to the dose indicator.
    • Hold the pen upright. Gently tap it as this will bring air bubbles to the top.
    • Push the dose knob all the way in until it stops and count slowly to 5. You’ll see 0 icon in the dose window.
    • Check for a drop at the needle tip. If you do not see the medicine drop, repeat the steps in this section.
    • If you still do not see the drop, change the needle and repeat the steps in this section.

  • How do I take the injection?

    • Inject under the skin of your stomach (abdomen) or upper leg (thigh).
    • If someone else is giving the injection, it can also be done in the back of your upper arm.
    • Inject into a different area of the body every week.
    • If you plan to stick to the same area, choose a different injection site within that area.
    • Turn the dose knob until the “1” icon appears. Now insert the needle and push the knob in completely until it stops.
    • Count to 5 while holding the dose knob. The 0 icon will appear in the dose window. Remove the needle from your skin.
    • Dispose of the used needle in a sharps container. DO NOT SHARE or REUSE it.
    • Discard the pen after 4 doses or 30 days from first use, whichever comes first.

  • What dosages are available?

    Our injections are available in 6 doses:

    • 2.5 mg
    • 5 mg
    • 7.5 mg
    • 10 mg
    • 12.5 mg
    • 15 mg

    Your dose is increased gradually to let your body adjust to the medication.

  • What if I miss a dose?

    If you miss a dose:

    • Take the missed dose as soon as possible, or at least within 4 days (96 hours) after the missed dose.
    • If more than 4 days have passed, skip the missed dose and take your next dose as per the original schedule.
    • Do not take two doses within 3 days of each other.

  • How do I increase dosages?

    We will automatically increase the dosage after each month (if you and our partner doctors deem it suitable).

    • You must start on the 2.5 mg dose.
    • After one month, you can progress to the 5 mg dose.
    • If changes in your weight are noticeable, your doctor will keep you on the 5 mg dose, also known as the maintenance dose.
    • However, if changes are not seen, your doctor will gradually increase your dosage to 7.5 mg, 10 mg, 12.5 mg, or 15 mg every four weeks.

  • Can I change my dosage?

    Do not alter your dose unless you have been instructed by our partner doctor to do so. You cannot exceed a dose of 15 mg per week. If you take more than you should, contact us as soon as possible.

  • How do I store the injection?

    • Store the injection in a refrigerator between 2°C - 8°C.
    • Alternatively, store it at room temperature, below 30°C, for up to 21 days.
    • Do not freeze the pen. DO NOT use if it has been frozen.
    • Keep the injection in the original package and protect it from direct light.

  • What are the benefits of taking the injection?

    The main benefit is that it's proven to effectively cause weight loss. When combined with a healthy lifestyle, individuals using our treatments can lose up to 21% of their original body weight.

  • What are the possible side effects?

    Like all prescription medicines, our injection too can cause some side effects. This is especially true during the first few months. However, the most common side effects are mild and go away on their own.

    Common side effects include:

    • Nausea (feeling sick)
    • Vomiting (being sick)
    • Indigestion and general stomach discomfort
    • Bloating and gas (flatulence)
    • Constipation
    • Inflammation of the stomach (gastritis)

    Most of these side effects should disappear once your body has gotten used to the medication. However, if you experience any persistent or worrying side effects, speak to us or your healthcare provider.
