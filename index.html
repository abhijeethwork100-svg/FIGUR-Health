Simple Process

Achieving your goals is as easy as 1-2-3

Our streamlined process gets you from your first question to your first prescription in as little as 48 hours — all from the comfort of your home.

1

Answer a short medical quiz

Share a few details about your health history so our doctors can confirm our treatment is safe and right for you. Takes just 5 minutes.

Doctor reviews & prescribes

A licensed Indian doctor reviews your answers and prescribes the most suitable GLP-1 medication for your needs.
