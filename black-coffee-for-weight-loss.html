What is black coffee?

Simply put, in its purest form, black coffee is a coffee with no milk, sugar, or frills. Just water and coffee grounds blended together to create a straightforward drink.

The best part? It's incredibly low in calories - by this, we mean just 2 calories per cup!

No wonder it's the next viral trend among those watching their waistlines! The country that once primarily sipped chai is now embracing the bold flavours of Arabica and Robusta with open arms.

Now that we know what black coffee is, it is time to explore how the bitter beverage can help you lose weight.

How does black coffee help in weight loss?

Black coffee is a multi-hyphenated beverage. Not only does it improve overall health, but it actually also acts as a natural fat burner.

Here is how you can lose weight with black coffee:

  • Reduces calorie intake:
    Black coffee is low in calories; with just 2 calories in a cup, it helps you cut down on calorie intake.
  • Boosts metabolism:
    Caffeine helps your body burn more calories by increasing your metabolic rate by 3-11%[1], but only briefly.
  • Uses fat for fuel:
    Black coffee makes your body use energy to break down fat and use it for energy. Chlorogenic acid* in the coffee slows down new fat cells from forming.
  • Curbs hunger:
    Caffeine might also help suppress your appetite and stop you from unnecessary snacking.
  • Improves workouts:
    Caffeine helps you exercise better by boosting endurance and delaying fatigue. It improves speed and makes muscles work better.

*CGA is a natural polyphenol compound found in plants, especially in coffee beans.

To put it briefly, you can have black coffee for weight loss. But keep in mind, just black coffee will not help you lose weight. You must pair it with diet and exercise to cut down on weight.

FAQs

  • How do you reduce belly fat with black coffee?

    Black coffee alone won't target belly fat, but it can support your overall fat-loss efforts. Combine it with a healthy diet and exercise for the best results.

  • Can I drink coffee every day for weight loss?

    Yes, you can, as long as you're not loading it with sweeteners, sugar and cream. Stay consistent and combine it with healthy lifestyle habits, including nutritious meals and regular workouts.

  • Is black coffee good for skin?

    Yes! Coffee contains antioxidants that protect your skin from damage and help slow ageing. It can also reduce dark spots and offer some sun protection.

    Its anti-inflammatory properties soothe conditions like acne, and it can improve skin elasticity.

  • Can we drink black coffee during periods?

    Caffeine narrows blood vessels in the uterus, which can restrict blood flow and make cramps worse. It also stimulates your nervous system, and increases anxiety and tension.

    The dehydrating action of coffee can intensify the bloating and cramps. If coffee makes things worse for you, try swapping it for an herbal tea or a coffee alternative during your period.

  • Can I add artificial sweeteners to my black coffee without affecting its weight loss benefits?

    While artificial sweeteners won't add calories, they might mess with your body in other ways.

    Some studies suggest they can increase cravings and might affect your gut bacteria. If you really can't stand black coffee, try weaning off sweeteners gradually.

  • Is cold-brew black coffee more effective for weight loss than hot-brewed coffee?

    Both cold brew and hot coffee can help with weight loss, but they have differences.

    Cold brew often has more caffeine if brewed strongly. This way, it helps burn fat better and improves your metabolism.

    Hot coffee delivers a faster caffeine hit and more antioxidants.

    The low acidic content of cold brew makes it gentler on the stomach, so the choice really comes down to what works best for you.

  • Does decaf black coffee have the same weight loss benefits as regular black coffee?

    The truth is, many of coffee's weight loss benefits come from caffeine.

    Due to its chlorogenic acid and appetite-suppressing properties, Decaf black coffee can still support weight loss.

    But it’s not quite as effective as regular black coffee since it’s missing that caffeine kick.

    If you’re sensitive to caffeine or just prefer a gentler option, decaf might be the better fit. Ultimately, it all comes down to what works best for you and your diet.

  • How does black coffee compare to green tea for weight loss?

    It's not a competition; both can be great for weight loss. Coffee typically has more caffeine than green tea, which might give you more advantages.

    Green tea has catechins and other antioxidants that could help burn fat. Instead of picking one, you can alternate between them for the best of both worlds.

  • Are there any specific types of coffee beans that are better for weight loss?

    Any coffee, green or black, can help with weight loss. Green coffee beans, rich in chlorogenic acids, may reduce fat absorption and increase metabolism.

    Black coffee's higher caffeine content can increase fat burning and suppress appetite. But they're most effective when combined with a healthy diet and exercise.

    Also, freshly roasted, properly brewed beans will taste better, meaning you're less likely to want to add calorie-laden creamers or sweeteners.

  • Does black coffee have any impact on mood?

    Yes. Because of its caffeine content, drinking black coffee often has favourable effects on mood. If taken in moderate amounts, black coffee makes one more alert and increases problem-solving skills. It also lowers the severity of the symptoms associated with depression.

    But, drinking caffeine in excess can manifest as increased anxiety or jitteriness.

  • Can we drink black coffee on an empty stomach for weight loss?

    Yes, some people may see weight loss by drinking black coffee on an empty stomach. Black coffee (especially without sugar or cream) is low in calories and contains caffeine. Caffeine can increase metabolism and enhance fat burning temporarily. It may also suppress your appetite for a while, which again reduces the calories you consume.

    However, some people may experience stomach irritation or increased hunger after drinking black coffee this way. If you have acid reflux or other digestive issues, consult your doctor before you add this to your weight loss plan.
