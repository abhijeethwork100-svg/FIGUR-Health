Summary: Figur Health collects only what is necessary to provide your programme. We do not sell your data. We use industry-standard security. You have full rights to access, correct, or delete your information at any time.

1. About This Policy

Figur Health ("Website") is an internet-based platform operated by Figur Health Private Limited ("Company", "we", "our", or "us"), incorporated under the provisions of the Companies Act, 2013. This Privacy Policy is published in compliance with:

  • Section 43A of the Information Technology Act, 2000 ("IT Act"); and
  • Information Technology (Reasonable Security Practices and Procedures and Sensitive Personal Information) Rules, 2011 ("SPDI Rules").

By accepting this Privacy Policy, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to the practices and policies outlined herein and consent to our collection, use, sharing, and disclosure of your information as described below.

We reserve the right to change, modify, add or delete portions of this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of our Website after any such changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

2. Information We Collect

Registration Information

When you register to use our Website, we collect your registration details including phone number, email address, and name. We verify your phone number using a one-time password (OTP) sent to your device.

Order Information

We may collect information necessary to process your order, including items you place in the cart, your postal address, and specific payment transaction details. We do not collect bank account numbers, debit or credit card details, or any sensitive payment instrument information — all payments are processed through third-party PCI-DSS compliant payment gateways.

Health Information

To facilitate your medical consultation and prescription, we collect relevant health information including your medical history, weight, height, and any existing conditions. This information is shared only with the licensed medical practitioners and pharmacies involved in your care.

Usage Data & Cookies

We collect standard web analytics data (pages visited, time spent, device type, browser) and use cookies to improve your experience. You may disable cookies via your browser settings, although this may affect site functionality. We use session cookies (deleted when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (to remember your preferences).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Process and fulfil your orders for medication and consultations;
  • Verify your identity and eligibility for our programme;
  • Communicate updates, delivery notifications, and clinical follow-ups;
  • Improve our platform, services, and user experience;
  • Comply with applicable legal and regulatory requirements;
  • Prevent fraud and ensure the security of our platform.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information with:

  • Licensed medical practitioners — to facilitate your clinical consultation and prescription;
  • Registered pharmacies — to dispense and deliver your prescribed medication;
  • Payment processors — strictly for the purpose of processing transactions;
  • Logistics partners — to deliver your orders;
  • Regulatory authorities — when required by law.

All third-party partners are contractually obligated to maintain the confidentiality and security of your information.

5. Data Security

We employ industry-standard physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Our platform uses SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission.

While we take all reasonable measures, no internet-based service can guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach affecting your rights, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as is necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations (typically 7 years for medical records as required by Indian regulations). You may request deletion of non-medical personal data at any time, subject to applicable legal requirements.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you;
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information;
  • Request deletion of your personal data (subject to legal retention requirements);
  • Withdraw consent to processing (which may affect your ability to use the service);
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@figurhealth.com.

8. Third-Party Links

Our Website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies independently.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a minor, please contact us immediately.
