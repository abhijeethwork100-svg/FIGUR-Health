Patient Responsibility & Compliance Agreement

By using this platform, undertaking an online medical consultation, and proceeding with treatment pursuant to a prescription issued by a registered medical practitioner, I affirm and declare truthfully as follows:

1. Voluntary Teleconsultation & Consent

I confirm that I am voluntarily opting for a teleconsultation through the Figur Health platform. I consent to share my medical information digitally and understand that the consultation and any prescription will be provided by an independent, registered medical practitioner based on the information I supply. I acknowledge that teleconsultation has inherent limitations compared to an in-person examination, and that any medicines prescribed will be dispensed by a registered third-party pharmacy.

2. Age and Capacity

I confirm that I am at least 18 years of age and an adult of sound mind and judgment, legally capable of entering into this agreement.

3. Legal Compliance

I am permitted by the laws of my country of residence to receive the treatment and medication that I am requesting for my personal use. I enter into this Agreement in full compliance with the laws of India and my country of residence.

4. Recent Medical Examination

I have recently undergone an examination with a local registered doctor who has evaluated my current condition and past medical history and deemed my health to be at a satisfactory level for the requested treatment.

5. Ongoing Relationship with Local Doctor

My local doctor is a registered medical practitioner who is available for a further consultation should the need arise. I agree to immediately contact my local doctor in the event of any side effects, complications, or queries regarding the medication. I acknowledge that the prescribing doctor on this platform and the dispensing pharmacy may also be contacted via the platform, and I agree to respond promptly to any outreach from them.

6. Personal Use Only

The prescription and medication dispensed to me are entirely for my own personal medical needs and personal use. I confirm that the medication will not be sold, transferred, or supplied to any third party, and that I will not stockpile medication beyond an adequate personal supply.

7. Complementary, Not Replacement, Care

I understand that the purpose of this service is to support, not replace, the relationship with my primary healthcare provider. I agree to keep my local doctor informed of any new medications, treatments, or supplements I am taking through this platform.

8. Accurate Information

I confirm that all information I have provided — including my medical history, current medications, allergies, and personal details — is accurate, complete, and truthful. I understand that providing false or misleading information may result in inappropriate prescribing, serious harm to my health, and may constitute fraud.

9. Monitoring and Compliance

I agree to:

  • Follow the dosing instructions provided by my prescribing doctor and the dispensing pharmacy;
  • Attend scheduled follow-up consultations and check-ins as recommended by my clinical team;
  • Report any adverse effects, changes in health status, or concerns to my doctor promptly;
  • Not adjust my dose without medical guidance.

10. Limitation of Platform Liability

I understand and accept that Figur Health is a digital platform that facilitates connections between patients and independent medical practitioners and pharmacies. Figur Health is not responsible for clinical decisions made by the independent practitioners or for the dispensing decisions made by the pharmacies.

11. Emergency Situations

I understand that telehealth services are not suitable for medical emergencies. In the event of a medical emergency, I will contact the local emergency services (112 in India) or proceed to my nearest emergency facility immediately and not rely on this platform for urgent care.

Declaration

By proceeding with the Figur Health programme, I confirm that I have read, understood, and agree to all of the above. I acknowledge this agreement is binding and forms part of the overall Terms & Conditions of the Figur Health platform.

If you have any questions about this agreement, please contact us at support@figurhealth.com before proceeding.
