Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: June 2024

Key points: You can cancel before dispatch. Refunds go back to your original payment method in 5–7 days. We do not accept returns, but will send replacements for wrong, missing, damaged, or expired products (within 7 days of delivery).

1. How to Cancel Your Order

You may cancel your order at any time before it has been dispatched from our pharmacy partner.

To cancel, please contact our Customer Service team as soon as possible:

  • Email: support@figurhealth.com
  • Phone: +91 96649 24546
  • Hours: Monday–Friday, 10:30–19:30 IST

Once your cancellation is confirmed, a full refund will be processed to your original payment method.

2. Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed as follows:

  • Refund is initiated by Figur Health within 1–2 business days of cancellation confirmation;
  • The refund will be credited to your original payment source (UPI, debit/credit card, net banking, etc.) within 5–7 business days after initiation, depending on your bank or payment provider;
  • You will receive an email and/or SMS notification when the refund has been initiated, including a refund reference ID.

3. Tracking Your Refund

You will receive notifications at each stage:

  • When your cancellation request is confirmed;
  • When the refund has been processed and a reference ID issued by our payment partner;
  • We recommend contacting your bank if the refund does not appear within 7 business days of the reference ID notification.

4. Returns Policy

For safety and regulatory reasons, we are unable to accept the return of any medication once it has been dispatched and delivered to you. This is in compliance with pharmaceutical regulations governing the handling of prescription medications in India.

5. Replacements

While we cannot accept returns, we will arrange a full replacement at no cost in the following circumstances — provided the replacement request is raised within 7 days of delivery:

  1. Wrong product delivered — you received a different dose or medication than prescribed;
  2. Missing products — items are missing from your order;
  3. Damaged products delivered — the packaging or product is visibly damaged;
  4. Expired products delivered — the product has passed its expiry date.

To raise a replacement request, please contact us at support@figurhealth.com or +91 96649 24546. Our team may request photographic evidence before processing the replacement. Once approved, a new order ID will be generated and will be visible in your order history.

6. Orders Already Dispatched

Once an order has been dispatched by our pharmacy partner, it cannot be cancelled. If you no longer wish to receive the order, please follow our replacement process above if the product falls under a qualifying category upon receipt.

7. Subscription Orders

For monthly subscription (kit) orders, cancellation requests must be received at least 48 hours before your next scheduled dispatch date to avoid the next shipment being processed. Cancellations received after this window will take effect on the following month's order.

8. Contact Us

For all cancellation, refund, and replacement queries:

  • Email: support@figurhealth.com
  • Phone: +91 96649 24546
  • Hours: Monday–Friday, 10:30–19:30 IST
