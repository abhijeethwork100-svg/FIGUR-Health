What to expect

Your weight loss journey, month by month

Results vary by individual, but here's a general guide to what most Figur Health members experience throughout their programme.

Weeks 1–4
Getting started & first signs
Your body begins adapting to the medication. Most people notice a reduction in appetite within the first week. Early weight loss of 1–2 kg is common.
Month 2–3
Momentum builds
Appetite suppression becomes more consistent. Many members report 3–5 kg of weight loss by the end of month three. Energy levels start improving.
Month 4–6
Visible transformation
Clothing starts fitting differently. Most members see 6–9 kg of weight loss. Dosage is often adjusted here for continued progress.
Month 7–12
Sustained progress
Consistent week-on-week loss. Health markers like blood pressure and blood sugar often improve significantly. Total loss of 10–16 kg is typical.
Month 13–16
Goal achieved
Clinical trials show an average of 21% body weight reduction at 16 months. Our team transitions you to a maintenance plan to help keep the weight off long term.

Do I qualify for Figur Health?

Our programme is designed for adults with a BMI of 27 or above, or those with weight-related health conditions. Our medical quiz takes 5 minutes and tells you if you're a good fit.

✓ You may be eligible if you:
  • • Are 18 years or older
  • • Have a BMI of 27 or higher
  • • Have tried other weight loss methods
  • • Don't have certain medical contraindications
✗ Not suitable if you:
  • • Are pregnant or breastfeeding
  • • Have Type 1 diabetes
  • • Have a history of pancreatitis
  • • Are under 18 years of age
